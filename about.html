About ZGH Projects

Building Excellence Since 2016

Our Story

ZGH Projects (PTY) LTD was established in 2016 by Hermann Botha, bringing extensive construction experience to the South African market. We have developed into one of South Africa's leading, predominantly black-owned construction service providers with our head office strategically located in East London, Eastern Cape province.

The company has grown organically by re-investing its earnings to fund sustainable growth. We have successfully expanded our operations across all nine provinces of South Africa, completing projects in both remote rural areas and urban centers throughout Eastern Cape, Mpumalanga, KwaZulu Natal, North West, and Limpopo provinces.

A Truly South African Company

We believe in embracing all people of our nation - working together to bring true change. We put roofs over heads, but we also put work boots on feet. We believe in giving real opportunity to young South Africans, with genuine authority and responsibility. We believe in building our nation not only with bricks and mortar, but with the powerful spark that comes from unity.
