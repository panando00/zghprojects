ABOUT ZGH PROJECTS

A truly South African company, truly empowered. Building our nation with quality, integrity, and unity.

Our Story

ZGH Projects (Pty) Ltd was established in 2016 by Mr. Hermann Botha, who brings extensive construction experience to lead this potentially emerging construction company. What started as a vision to provide quality construction services has grown into one of South Africa's leading, predominantly black-owned construction service providers.

Based in East London in the Eastern Cape province of South Africa, with satellite offices in Kempton Park, Gauteng, we have expanded our reach across all provinces of the country. Our company is growing organically by re-investing earnings to fund sustainable growth and development.

We believe in embracing all people of our nation - working together to bring true change. We put roofs over heads, but we also put work boots on feet. We believe in giving real opportunity to young South Africans, with genuine authority and responsibility.

Leadership

Hermann Botha

Managing Director & Project Manager

Hermann Botha is the visionary founder and Managing Director of ZGH Projects. With extensive construction experience spanning over a decade, Hermann established ZGH Projects in 2016 with a mission to provide quality construction services across South Africa.

Under his leadership, ZGH Projects has grown from a small local contractor to a CIDB Level 4 registered company serving all nine provinces of South Africa. Hermann's commitment to empowerment, quality workmanship, and client satisfaction has been instrumental in establishing ZGH Projects as a trusted name in the construction industry.

His hands-on approach and technical expertise ensure that every project meets the highest standards of quality and professionalism. Hermann continues to drive the company's growth while maintaining strong relationships with clients and stakeholders across the country.

Skills Development

We actively encourage growth and development in all our staff and view skills development and capacity building as a critical component of our business.

We give the communities we work in the chance to learn practical skills related to building technology and construction rules and regulations. We budget approximately 5% of our total salary bill for training and development.

Training Areas:

  • Building technology
  • Site management
  • Engineering advancement programs
  • Construction-related certifications

Environmental Commitment

We serve the environment and preserve local heritage in conducting our business. We understand and monitor the effects of our operations on the environment.

Our designs and operations comply with national environmental standards, and we often exceed requirements to meet specific facility needs.
